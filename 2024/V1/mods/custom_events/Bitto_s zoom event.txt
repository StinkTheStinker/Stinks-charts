value 1 must be separated into 3 pieces, or else it will not work:
	1 - camera to zoom (camGame,camHUD or camOther)
	2 - zoom value (what to zoom into)
	3 - zoom duration (how long it takes to zoom)
	
ex. "camGame,2.1,0.5"

value 2 is just the tween ease you wanna use:
https://api.haxeflixel.com/flixel/tweens/FlxEase.html
